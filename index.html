01 Who I Am

3D Artist &
Creator

Hi! I'm Adrián — a 3D artist with a passion for modeling, sculpting, texturing and bringing digital worlds to life. Currently pursuing a Higher Degree in 3D Animation, Games & Interactive Environments.

My core is 3D: from high-poly character sculpts in ZBrush to environment design, product visualization and full VFX compositing pipelines. Beyond that, I've shipped games as a solo programmer and director, and I work fluently in Nuke and After Effects.

I'm driven by creative, ambitious projects — in games, animation, film or TV. If it moves, lights up a screen or makes people feel something, I want to build it.

Game Jam · Unity 3D

SlotVerse

+

SlotVerse is a game developed for a Game Jam, where each match offers a unique experience. Players spin a slot machine to randomly combine a game mode, enemy type, and setting, generating a different game scenario every time. The result can be anything from a chaotic top-down shooter to a high-speed 2.5D racing challenge, each with its own mechanics and enemy behaviors.

My Role 3D modeler and UI designer/programmer — created in-game assets and designed the interface logic, ensuring clarity and consistency across randomly generated game modes. Software Unity 3D | 2D · Photoshop · Autodesk Maya · Substance Painter
